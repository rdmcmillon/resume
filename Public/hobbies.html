My Hobbies

I use to be okay with just sharing a tent with my hubby, but I have been there-done that and now I enjoy "glamping" where I have my own bed! I grew up in Montana, training horses and enjoying the beautiful country around me, and I have a great appreciation for what God has created.

I have hobbies indoor as well. I love a challenge and am always looking for new things to learn to do. I spend most of my time on the computer and have visited enough websites that I recognize good websites from the more challenging websites. I am learning how to crochet and making something from a ball of yarn. My latest hobby is sublimation. I am always on the lookout for new things to sublimate. I love customizing using a personal photo oy adding a name to make a unique gift. Sometimes I can even get my husband to help out and we love working side by side. The whole sublimation process is fascinating and there is a lot to learn. I enjoy being creative - my art teacher would be shocked!
